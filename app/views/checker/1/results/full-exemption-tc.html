Based on what you've told us You get help with NHS costs

Because you get qualifying tax credits and have a household income of £15,276 or less:

You get free:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS dental check-ups and treatment
  • sight tests
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports

You get money off:

  • new glasses or contact lenses
  • repairing or replacing your glasses or contact lenses
  • travel for NHS treatment

Any children under 20 included on your tax credit claim are also entitled to the same help.

If you are pregnant or have children

  • As part of the Healthy Start scheme, if you're at least than ten weeks pregnant or have a child under the age of four, you may be entitled to:
  • £3.10 a week to spend on foods such as cow’s milk, baby formula, fresh or frozen fruit and vegetables
  • free vitamins

    • Find out more about who qualifies for Healthy Start

    How to get your help

    You don't have to do anything more, although you may have to show your Tax Credit Exemption Certificate to your pharmacist, optician or dentist.

    You can use your tax credit exemption certificate, this is a small plastic card that should have been sent to you.

    If you have lost it, you can use your latest tax credit award notice as proof.
