Based on what you've told us You get help with NHS costs

You get free:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS dental check-ups and treatment
  • NHS sight tests
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports

You get some money off the cost of:

  • new glasses and contact lenses
  • repairing or replacing your glasses and contact lenses
  • travel for NHS treatment

Your partner and any children under 20 included on your Universal Credit claim are also entitled to full help with NHS costs.

How to get your help

You may have to show proof you get that you get Universal Credit.

You can use your Universal Credit award notice as proof. Get a copy of yours by contacting the Jobcentre Plus Helpline.
