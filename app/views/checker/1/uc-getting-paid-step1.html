While you're waiting on your Universal Credit decision

Until your first assessment period is complete, Universal Credit doesn't entitle you to help with NHS costs.

If you need to pay for things like prescriptions or dental treatment, make sure you get a receipt and ask how you could claim a refund once you have your award notice.
